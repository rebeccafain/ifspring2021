Lemon Squares Recipe

When life gives you lemons, make lemon squares!

Lemon bars are a sweet, tangy, and of course lemony dessert bar. When well made, lemon bars will have a perfectly crumbly shortbread base with a gooey lemon topping almost like custard. A delicate dusting of powdered sugar acts as the perfect finishing touch! This is a quick and easy recipe for zesty lemon squares. These bittersweet treats are perfect to welcome in the spring season. Let's get started!


  • Summary

  • Yield: 1 - 9x13 inch pan
  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Cook: 40 mins
  • Total: 55 mins
  • Servings: 36

    • Ingredients

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 juiced lemons

    • Nutrition Facts

  • Per Serving:
  • 126 calories
  • 1.6 g protein
  • 17.8 g carbohydrates

  • 44.4 mg sodium

  • Instructions

  • Step 1
  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  • Step 2
  • In a medium bowl, blend together so!ened butter, 2 cups flour and 1/2 cup sugar. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13 inch pan.

  • Step 3
  • Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until firm and golden. In another bowl, whisk together the remaining 1 1/2 cups sugar and 1/4 cup flour. Whisk in the eggs and lemon juice. Pour over the baked crust.

  • Step 4
  • Bake for an additional 20 minutes in the preheated oven. The bars will firm up as they cool.
